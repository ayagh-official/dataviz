The plot depicts a comparative analysis of gender ratios within a sample size drawn from nationwide data concerning food poverty in a specific country. Nations exhibiting gender ratios ranging from 49% to 51% are considered balanced and are positioned centrally on the plot. On the left-hand side, countries with higher female ratios are represented, while those with higher male ratios under food poverty are indicated on the right-hand side with a square symbol. Additionally, as one ascends on the plot, the prevalence of severe food poverty increases, with nations situated on the upper portion experiencing more pronounced challenges in this regard.
